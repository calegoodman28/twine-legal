Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Twine ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Twine mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Phone number (used as your unique identifier), first name, date of birth, gender, and sexual preference.
  • Profile Information: Hometown, current city, occupation, university, and profile photos (up to 6 images).
  • Group Information: Group names, descriptions, group photos, and group location (city/neighborhood level).
  • Communications: Messages, images, videos, and GIFs sent through in-app chats, as well as message reactions.
  • Voting Activity: Your group's votes (like or skip) on other groups shown in your feed.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to help you set your group's location and connect with nearby groups. We only access location while the app is in use.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens for delivering notifications through Apple Push Notification Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To match your group with compatible groups based on preferences and location.
  • To enable group-based voting (like/skip) on your feed.
  • To facilitate messaging between matched groups.
  • To send push notifications about new matches, messages, likes, and activity.
  • To maintain and improve the App's functionality and performance.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information (first name, photos, age, and group details) is visible to other users through the feed, group profiles, and chat features. Your phone number is not displayed to other users.
  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, file storage, and push notifications. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Pause Profile: You can pause your profile at any time in Settings, which hides your profile from other users' feeds.
  • Delete Messages: Messages you send can be viewed by all participants in a chat.
  • Leave Groups: You can leave any group at any time through the group settings.
  • Delete Account: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Notification Preferences: You can manage push notification preferences in the App's settings or through your device's system settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. Chat messages are retained as long as the chat exists. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For phone number verification and account management.
  • Firebase Firestore: For storing user profiles, groups, chats, and voting data.
  • Firebase Storage: For storing photos and media files.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For delivering push notifications.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics tracking, or crash reporting services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
